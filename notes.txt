The major sections of the library are:

Fiction - This section contains novels, short stories, and other works of imaginative fiction.
Nonfiction - This section contains books on a wide range of subjects such as history, biography, science, philosophy, and more.
Reference - This section includes encyclopedias, dictionaries, atlases, and other reference resources for looking up information.
Children's - This section contains books, picture books, and other materials geared towards younger readers.
Periodicals - This section houses magazines, newspapers, and other serial publications.
Media - This section contains audio books, DVDs, CDs, and other multimedia resources.
Special Collections - Many libraries have specialized collections, such as rare books, local history materials, or archives.
